About me

My name is Can Silay. I'm 21 years old and a third-year student at Deltion College studying Creative Developer. I'm a programmer with two years of Unity experience.

I enjoy exploring different aspects of Unity development and am building a broad skill set across gameplay programming, systems design, and VR.

I like experimenting with different aspects of game development and immersion — from implementing a drawing mechanic in my fighting game, to developing a VR drum rhythm game, to creating a home server where people can upload levels made in my 3D nonogram editor.

My goal is to keep expanding my knowledge of game development while taking on new challenges that push my technical and creative skills.
